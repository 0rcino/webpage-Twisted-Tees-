Boys Over Flowers

Boys Over Flowers is the brand that embodies creativity, craft, and heart art. It represents our unique story as a group of guys passionate in creating permanent beauty through the idea inspired by roses-they are perfectly and intricately done with ribbons and crochet, symbolizing care, dedication, and uniqueness. Our mission is to bring far more than just the product with meaningful creations that celebrate self-expression and the beauty of handmade artistry. Each piece embodies, in itself, the strength and elegance that roses stand for, as well as its roots and passion.

Why Choose us?

Boys Over Flowers is a brand born of creativity and dedication to handcrafted artistry. We specialize in roses made out of ribbons and crochet in our quest to redefine elegance and infuse a unique touch on all our floral-inspired creations. Our products transcend simple designs and are expressions of individuality and craftsmanship, so carefully thought about to speak to the soul of anyone who treasures the heart of meaningful pieces. At Boys Over Flowers, we believe in making a combination of beauty, quality, and innovation in order to make items that will make good impressions.
